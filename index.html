Conway's Game f Life

In this torus 2D grid, each cell as a "life" has 8 surrounding neighbors. Alive cells die in the next round if the number of their alive neighbors is strictly less than 2 or greater than 3. Dead cells would come back to life if they have exactly 3 alive neighbors. These simple rules yet produce many interesting patterns. You can try them out here and see the evolution.
